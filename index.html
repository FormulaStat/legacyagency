About Legacy Access

We’re a community-driven agency helping eligible citizens access official U.S. relief programs. Our mission is to make the process simple, secure, and accessible — especially for underserved families and individuals.

Programs You Can Apply For

  • 🏠 Emergency Housing Assistance
  • 💡 Utility & Energy Bill Support
  • 💼 Business Recovery & Start-up Grants
  • 🌪️ Disaster Recovery Relief
Start Application

What You Need to Apply

  • Valid Government-Issued ID
  • Proof of Address or Lease
  • Income Statement or Tax Return
  • Optional: Utility or Medical Bills

Be sure documents are scanned or photographed clearly. We’ll guide you during the upload process.
